About Me

I’m Md Jakir Hossain, a passionate graduate student in the Erasmus Mundus Copernicus Master in Digital Earth. My academic journey began with a focus on spatial planning with a strong interest in remote sensing, GIS, urban planning, environmental monitoring, and disaster management. Through my bachelor’s degree in Urban and Regional Planning at Jahangirnagar University, I gained hands-on experience in Geospatial tools. To further develop my skills, I am currently studying at Paris Lodron University Salzburg as part of the Copernicus Master’s program, specializing in Geovisualization. I aim to contribute in geospatial analysis to address challenges in climate resilience and sustainable urban development..
